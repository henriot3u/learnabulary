Import / Export your data



Your data such as lists and statistics about exams are stored in the storage of your browser. If you delete the storage, you will lost all your data. To save them and retrieve them everywhere, export them as a file. You will get them as one Json file. Please don't touch it. If you need your data again, just drag and drop the file below. If you want to export your lists to give them to a friend, we advise you to DESELECT "statistics" in order not to give him the words influenced by your success during your exams.
